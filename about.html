ABOUT US

Welcome to Cozy Yarn Academy ! This is your online destination for all things yarn. We welcome you to learn, connect, and create in a friendly space that we want to be just for crafters of all ages and experience levels.
Founded by passionate yarn enthusiasts, we share top-notch courses with a community that supports and encourages you as you explore knitting, crocheting, and more. Just starting out or perfecting some advanced techniques, we are here to inspire and guide you every step of the way.
Thanks for joining us on our creative journey. We are excited to see what you will make.

MISSION

Cozy Yarn Academy cultivates a nurturing community for yarn lovers to learn, grow, and create through excellent education in crafting.

VISION

We want to be the top online destination for yarn crafters that inspires a global community to take knitting and crocheting seriously, build a dynamic space which is warm and welcoming.

FEATURES

Diverse Courses

More classes in knitting and crocheting, from basic to the most advanced techniques that ensure you're prepared for all levels.

Flexible Learning

On-demand access to course materials, so you can learn at your own pace, anywhere, anytime.

Resource Library

A rich library of video tutorials, tips, and guides to help you in your crafting journey.

Personalized Support

Dedicated support from our team of supporters to help you overcome any questions or difficulties at each step.

Interactive Webinars

Latest trends, techniques, and industry insights are updated through engaging, live webinars with Q&A and discussion opportunities.

Contact Us

We’re here to help! Whether you have questions, feedback, or just want to chat about your latest project, don’t hesitate to reach out.
