About Us

Welcome to Cozy Yarn Academy! This is your online destination for all things yarn. We welcome you to learn, connect, and create in a friendly space made just for crafters of all ages and experience levels.

Founded by passionate yarn enthusiasts, we share top-notch courses with a community that supports and encourages you as you explore knitting, crocheting, and more.

Just starting out or perfecting advanced techniques — we're here to inspire and guide you every step of the way. We're excited to see what you'll make.
